About Us


Royal Garden Middle East is a leading consumer goods company based in Dubai, the United Arab Emirates. We specialize in the Food and Beverage products import, export distribution and marketing segment. Since our inception in 2016, we have always prided ourselves to only distributing high quality products to our customers through our extensive market reach that has been honed during the past few decades that started through our perfume manufacturing and distribution arm Natural Fragrances that was established in 2005 in Sharjah. The company started out by sourcing only the freshest fruits and vegetables from all over the world and marketing these to the wholesale and retail markets. We gradually moved into selling FMCG products such as Basmati Rice, Sunflower oil and etc under our own brands RG. Today we have grown into an entity that is world renowned and where we pride ourselves to be a company that only provides high quality consumer products at value for money. We firmly believe in our motto “A company is only good enough as it’s people” Only their commitment and loyalty can deliver the service and performance our stake holders have come to expect from us. Our employees are highly motivated through the possibilities of personal development and by the results related financial rewards. We are a company that believes that we are all crew. There are no passengers. Our strategy has always been to forge progressive, profitable, and healthy partnerships with principals who are leaders in their sectors. Our Senior Management Team brings many years of entrepreneurial experience to adding value to these relationships.
